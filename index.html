Our Programs

Age-appropriate programs designed to support development at every stage of early childhood.

Infant Care (12 months - 18 months)

Gentle, individualized care that supports bonding, sensory development, and early milestones.

Toddler Program (18 months - 3 years)

Language development, social skills, and independence through structured play and exploration.

Preschool (3 - 5 years)

School readiness through literacy, math, science, and social-emotional learning activities.

Kindergarten Care (5 - 7 years)

Homework help, enrichment activities, and safe supervision for school-aged children.
